Heat transfer (base unit watt per square meter kelvin, kg · s⁻³ · K⁻¹).

Heat transfer is also known as heat transfer coefficient, film coefficient, or film effectiveness. Commonly expressed using derived units power and area: watt per square meter kelvin.

Heat transfer has the same kind as temperature interval, as this quantity relates to change of temperature. Not of kind TemperatureKind, used by thermodynamic temperature. See thermodynamic temperature for a full explanation.
